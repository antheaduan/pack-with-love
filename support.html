Fund a backpack set

Every complete set includes the four core items — a backpack, coloring book, T-shirt, and coloring set — ready to be packed and shared with a child in Senegal.

Our first-year goal is at least 100 complete sets, growing toward 1,000 as support allows. Gifts of every size move us closer.

♥ Donate Now Ask About Giving
A note on giving: All donations are received and managed through ACO's approved financial channels. Pack with Love students and advisors never personally collect or hold funds. Whether a gift is tax-deductible depends on the receiving organization and applicable law.

Ways to help

  • Fund one or more complete backpack sets
  • Sponsor a village's worth of sets
  • Contribute approved supplies or handmade items
  • Offer a professional service (design, printing, shipping)
  • Share the project with friends, family, and your church

Not sure how to help? Reach out and we'll find the right fit.

For students & families

Join the club

Pack with Love is designed primarily for elementary and middle school students who want to use creativity, teamwork, and compassion to serve children. Students may help design, create, pack, fundraise, or share the story — in age-appropriate roles with adult guidance.

Students may join with parent or guardian permission. Because many members are young, parent involvement is an important and welcomed part of participation. Membership never requires a financial contribution.

Ask About Joining →

What members can do

  • Design artwork, coloring pages, and materials
  • Help prepare and pack items
  • Create flyers, videos, and project updates
  • Write thank-you messages to supporters
  • Reflect on each project and help improve the next
For churches, schools & nonprofits

Partner with us

We collaborate project by project with churches, VBS and children's ministries, schools, nonprofits, and mission programs that serve children and families. Each partnership is project-specific, and we listen carefully to your needs before we design anything.

We're glad to support the relationships and programs you already lead — never to replace them.

Start a Conversation →

How partnership works

  • You share the children's needs and setting
  • We design a theme and the core items together
  • Adult Advisors handle finances, production & logistics
  • Items are distributed through your trusted program
  • We reflect together and improve future projects
