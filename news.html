LatestJuly 2026

Fundraising begins for our first 100 backpacks

We've reached an exciting milestone: with the backpack and coloring book designs complete, Pack with Love is beginning to raise support for our very first goal — at least 100 complete backpack sets for children in Senegal, growing toward 1,000 as support allows.

Every gift helps a child open a backpack made just for them. Thank you for being part of the very first chapter of our story.
