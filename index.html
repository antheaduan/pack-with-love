What we do

Creativity that becomes care

Every Pack with Love project gives a child something useful to carry, something joyful to create, and a reminder that they are welcomed and valued.

🎒

Color-it-yourself backpacks

Our signature gift: a child-friendly backpack with a theme-based coloring design on the front that each child can personalize and make their own.

🖍️

Coloring books & care items

A matching coloring book, T-shirt, coloring set, and encouragement items complete each thoughtful package of love, joy, and hope.

🌱

Growing young leaders

Students listen, design, create, and serve in age-appropriate roles — growing in creativity, teamwork, and compassion with adult guidance.

Our first project

The Love & Hope Backpack Project

Our founding project supports ACO's Hope and Care Program during the 2027 Annual Medical Mission Outreach in Senegal. We're preparing complete, child-centered backpack sets to be packed at the ACO base and shared with children across local villages.

Each set is built around four core items — a backpack, a coloring book, a T-shirt, and a coloring set — designed to belong together as one gift of love.
